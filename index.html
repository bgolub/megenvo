About

Megen Vo is a Bay Area soprano with a voice spanning operatic repertoire, operetta, and musical theater. She has performed with Opera San Jose, West Bay Opera, the Lamplighters Music Theatre, Lyric Theatre San Jose, and South Bay Musical Theatre, appearing in roles ranging from Puccini to Gilbert & Sullivan to Cole Porter.

A prize-winner at the Cal-Western and SF Bay Area NATS auditions and the Arizona International Music Competition, Megen has earned top honors in both classical and musical theater categories. She brings to the stage not only technical range — F3 to D6 — but a musicianship shaped by years of study with teachers including Erin McOmber, Julia Nielsen, and Amy Heumann.

Off the stage, Megen is a practicing Pediatrician and holds a Doctor of Medicine from the University of Rochester School of Medicine and a B.A. in voice and psychology from Case Western Reserve University.
